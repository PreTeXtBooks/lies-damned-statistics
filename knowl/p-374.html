If, as before, event \(A\) consists of those outcomes having property \(X\) and \(B\) is those with property \(Y\text{,}\) the event \(A\cap B\) will consist of those outcomes which satisfy both \(X\) and \(Y\text{.}\) In other words, “\(A\cap B\)” can be described as “\(A\) and \(B\text{.}\)”
