Suppose your friend Mohammad Wang is an excellent dart-player. He throws at a board and you measure the \(x\)-coordinate of where the dart goes, as in Checkpoint 4.4.10 with the center corresponding to \(x=0\text{.}\) You notice that his darts are rarely — only 5% of the time in total! — more than \(5cm\) from the center of the board.
