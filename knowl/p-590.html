Let \(A\) be the event “the car had to stop at the \(1^{\text{st}}\) light” and \(B\) be the event “the car had to stop at the \(2^{\text{nd}}\) light.” What else would you have to count, over your year of data collection, to estimate the probabilities of \(A\) and of \(B\text{?}\) Pick some numbers for all of these variables and show what the probabilities would then be.
